## Discovery Questions

### Where: Infrastructure & Access

- Where does the first workflow's data live?
- Which systems must AI read from or act on?
- Do those systems expose APIs, MCP-compatible interfaces, exports, or databases?
- Who owns identity, permissions, retention, and audit logs?
- Are there latency, data residency, or local inference constraints?
- Which deployment path is plausible: NooX, customer cloud, NNGTs cloud, or hybrid?

### What: Applications & Agents

- Which repeated workflow creates visible cost, delay, risk, or revenue impact?
- What should the AI app or agent actually do?
- What should it never do?
- What context, tools, documents, and systems does it need?
- What is the right autonomy level: read-only, approved actions, writeback, or multi-system automation?
- Where should humans interact with it: chat, dashboard, internal app, email, CRM, ticketing, or another surface?

### How: Operations & Impact

- Who is the executive sponsor?
- Who owns the workflow day to day?
- Who approves sensitive decisions?
- What metric proves value: cost, cycle time, quality, revenue, risk, or control?
- What baseline exists today?
- Who will operate, monitor, and improve the system after launch?

## Objection Handling

### "We already use ChatGPT or Copilot."

Good. NovaOrbit starts where individual AI tools stop: company memory, source-system access, workflow ownership, approvals, deployment boundary, and measurable evidence.

### "We are not ready for implementation."

That is why NovaOrbit exists. The assessment identifies what must be true before implementation, and it prevents expensive false starts.

### "Can we just do a workshop?"

A workshop can create alignment. NovaOrbit creates a scored maturity baseline, red-gate blockers, first-workflow decision, and deployment path.

### "Is this a technical audit?"

Standard is not a deep technical audit. It is a maturity assessment. In-Depth adds deeper stack, access, API/MCP, governance, and ROI validation.

### "Why Nova Nuggets?"

Because Nova Nuggets can assess, build, deploy, and run the full stack: NooX infrastructure, customer-cloud or NNGTs-cloud deployment, ZAKI agents, AI apps, automation, governance, and operating support.
