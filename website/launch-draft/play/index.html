( Questions? Answered )
What is ZAKI?+
ZAKI is an AI product family for your digital life. Agent and Spaces are available now for visible work and project context. Minutes and Design are coming next.
Is ZAKI just chat?+
No. Chat is one interface. ZAKI is built around an agent and a brain layer: it can remember, reason across context, track goals, and help move work forward instead of only replying.
What products are in the family?+
Agent and Spaces are available now. Minutes and Design are coming next. Agent writes the personal Brain; Spaces keeps a separate project-scoped store.
What does it cost?+
Free, then Personal, Pro, and Pro MAX at $15, $45, and $99 USD per month. Paid plans increase the shared weekly and burst capacity shown in the app.
Who builds ZAKI?+
ZAKI is built by Nova Nuggets. The public product name is ZAKI; Nova Nuggets is the company behind the product family.
