( Privacy is part of the product )

The transcript is not your Brain.

Minutes keeps raw meeting material inside the meeting surface with a visible retention state. Only governed, useful distillates such as a decision or action can later become part of Agent memory. Verbatim transcripts do not enter Personal Brain.

( From capture to follow-through )

See the meeting become useful.

The meeting state, transcript, extracted decisions, actions, and draft follow-up stay inspectable. A failed processing run keeps the transcript intact and exposes a retry instead of silently losing the result.
