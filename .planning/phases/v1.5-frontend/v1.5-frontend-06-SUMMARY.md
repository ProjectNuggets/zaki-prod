## What Was Built

**Route:** `/brain` registered as a direct child of the App route in `src/routes.tsx` — same pattern as PricingPage and HelpPage, not routed through ChatArea. ErrorBoundary from App.tsx wraps it for free.

**BrainPage:** Full shell with:
- `useBrainGraph(userId)` fetch from `@/queries`
- `SkeletonBrainPage` shown while loading
- `BrainEmptyState` rendered when `total_nodes_in_corpus === 0`
- `BrainSemanticDegradedBanner` rendered when `semantic_degraded === true` (dismissible)
- Timeline/Graph tabs (Timeline default) with `data-testid="brain-timeline-slot"` and `data-testid="brain-graph-slot"` placeholders

**i18n:** `brain.*`, `sidebar.brain`, and `common.dismiss` keys added to both `en.json` and `ar.json`.

**Skeleton:** `SkeletonBrainPage` appended to `src/app/components/ui/skeleton.tsx` following the `SkeletonMemoryViewer` pattern.

## Decisions Made

- `/brain` is a standalone route child of App (not wrapped in ChatArea) — consistent with other full-page routes like PricingPage
- Tab slot content is intentionally placeholder text with testid anchors — plans 07 and 08 will replace with real timeline and graph components
- Empty state migration CTA navigates to `/?import=memories` as a temporary hook — plan 09/10 will wire to the real import panel
